Privacy Policy

TeleCue does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

All content created or processed within the app — including text, audio, or images — is handled entirely on the user’s device and is never transmitted to external servers.

TeleCue does not use analytics, tracking technologies, advertising SDKs, or third-party services.

Since no personal data is collected, users are not required to create accounts, provide consent, or request access to, correction of, or deletion of personal data.
